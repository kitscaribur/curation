[Interview] Panel discussion with Hello Dump

As a part of the show "The Wall is Never Innocent, Unless..." curated by Bowen Zhang and Jack Kinsman. Hello Dump presents a panel discussion on the DIY culture in London art scene and what it means to be a young artist in the current climate.

Presenting artists: Jack Kinsman / Bowen Zhang / Lorenzo Bucci

Presenting curator: Nina Wong

Hosted by: Chaney Diao from Hello Dump
