About me

Portuguese-born Macanese independent curator.

Nina's curatorial practice revolves around the fictional world-building and its relationship with humans. Currently based in London, her curatorial practice aims to bring more young artists to attention and challenge the traditional viewer and art relationship by using unconventional spaces and introducing more interactive pieces.
